Who You'll Meet 你将遇见的人

Zhang Kuo (Kuo Zhang)
President, Alibaba.com
He's been leading the original Alibaba platform — launched in 1999 — for nearly a decade. A $30 trillion global B2B marketplace serving 50 million businesses across 200 countries, now being reinvented with AI.

Ask him about the "one-person company" wave: 30–40% of his platform's customers are now solo entrepreneurs powered entirely by AI. What does that mean for the future of work?
阿里国际站总裁，领导着1999年创立的阿里巴巴原平台近十年。服务200个国家5000万企业的全球B2B市场，正被AI重新定义。
Zheng Bo (Bo Zheng)
CTO, Taotian Group · Architect of Happy Horse
Earlier this year, an anonymous AI video model appeared out of nowhere and topped every benchmark. The global AI community spent weeks trying to figure out who built it.

It was his team. Come ask him the backstory — and what "Happy Horse" actually means. Also the mind behind "Happy Oyster" and Token Fundary.
淘天集团CTO，"Happy Horse"和"Happy Oyster"的技术架构师——今年初一个匿名AI视频模型横扫所有榜单，全球AI社区花了几周猜测背后团队，就是他。
Lily Yan (Yan Xiaolei)
CEO, Freshippo (Hema)
She's reimagining grocery retail with AI — where prediction errors aren't academic; they're measured in tons of spoiled salmon. If you think AI in the real world is just chatbots, she'll change your mind.
盒马CEO，用AI重塑生鲜零售——预测误差不是论文里的数字，是按吨计算的三文鱼损耗。
Bo Liu (Liu Bo)
Leader, Tmall · Alimama · Taobao Live
Commerce at planetary scale: 900M+ monthly active users, AI-powered search that understands "what should I wear to my ex's wedding," and a live-streaming commerce engine that processes more transactions in an hour than most startups do in a year.
天猫、阿里妈妈、淘宝直播负责人。9亿月活用户，AI驱动的搜索引擎和直播电商。
Lillian Jiang
CPO, Alibaba E-Commerce Business Group
She designs the talent and organizational architecture for a company operating at planetary scale. She can tell you what a real AI career path looks like — and where the organizational bottlenecks are when you're trying to become "AI Native."
阿里巴巴电商事业群CPO，设计着星球级规模企业的人才和组织架构。能告诉你真正的AI职业路径长什么样。
Tingxiang Wang, Ding Jian, Cao Jiayin
Business Line Leaders, Taotian Group
The leaders behind fast fashion, recommendation systems, and business strategy at platforms processing billions of AI-powered requests daily. They manage the algorithms that decide what a billion people see when they open their phones.
淘天集团快时尚、推荐事业部及业务战略负责人。管理着每天处理数十亿AI请求的推荐算法。
